üéì PAANO MAGSIMULA NG RESEARCH?

üìñ Ano ba ang Research?

Ang research ay parang investigative work kung saan ikaw ay magsasaliksik tungkol sa isang topic, mag-aaral mula sa ibang sources, magsasagawa ng experiment o survey, at makakagawa ng conclusion based sa nakuha mong data. Para siyang paggawa ng detective report pero sa academic way!

Halimbawa ng topic: "Effectiveness of Gamified Learning Platform for HTML and CSS Education among Grade 11 Students"

STEP 0: Preparation

üéØ Bago Magsimula ng Kahit Ano

1. Pumili ng Topic/Research Idea

  • Dapat something na interesting sa'yo
  • May practical use o relevance
  • May available na resources (books, internet, respondents)
  • Hindi masyadong broad, hindi rin masyadong narrow

‚úÖ GOOD Topic:

"Gamified Web Platform for HTML Learning among Grade 11 STEM Students"

Bakit good? Specific, may target population, measurable, relevant

‚ùå BAD Topic:

"Technology in Education"

Bakit bad? Too broad, walang specific focus, mahirap sukatin

2. Mag-research ng Related Studies

Bago ka mag-finalize ng topic, check mo muna kung:

  • May available ba na studies related sa topic mo?
  • Recent ba ang mga studies (5-10 years old)?
  • May gap ba na pwede mong i-address?
üí° Tip: Use Google Scholar, ResearchGate, o ask your teacher for sources!

3. Humingi ng Guidance sa Research Adviser

  • Ipresent ang idea mo
  • Magtanong kung feasible ba (kaya mo ba gawin?)
  • Ask for suggestions on improvement
STEP 1: Proposal Presentation

üìù Gumawa ng Concept Paper

Ano ang Concept Paper? Ito ay short version ng research proposal mo. Parang "preview" kung ano ang plano mong gawin.

Mga Kasama sa Concept Paper:

Part Ano ang Ilalagay
Research Title Clear, specific title ng study mo
Proponents Pangalan ng lahat ng group members
Rationale BAKIT mo ginagawa ang study? Ano ang problema?
Purpose Statement ANO ang goal ng research mo?
Theoretical Framework Anong theories ang base ng study mo?
Conceptual Framework Diagram showing INPUT ‚Üí PROCESS ‚Üí OUTPUT
Research Design PAANO mo gagawin? (Experimental, Survey, etc.)
Statement of the Problem Mga specific questions na sasagutin mo

Ano ang Mangyayari:

  • Ipresent mo sa adviser mo lang
  • Adviser will give feedback at recommendations
  • I-revise mo based sa suggestions
‚ö†Ô∏è Note: Informal pa ito, walang formal panel. Relax lang!
STEP 2: Title Defense

üé§ Ipresent sa Panel of Examiners

Ano ang Title Defense? Dito mo ipepresent ang buong concept paper mo sa mga panelists (3 teachers usually) para aprubahan ang topic mo.

Mga Gagawin:

  • Prepare presentation (PowerPoint/Canva)
  • Explain clearly ang rationale, objectives, methodology
  • Ready ka sa questions ng panelists
  • Bring printed copies ng concept paper

Typical Questions ng Panelists:

‚ùì "Bakit mo napili ang topic na ito?"

‚ùì "Paano mo susukatin ang effectiveness?"

‚ùì "Sino ang target respondents?"

‚ùì "Anong theories ang gagamitin mo?"

‚ùì "Feasible ba ito given your resources?"

Output:

  • ‚úÖ APPROVED - Proceed to next stage with recommendations
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è CONDITIONAL - May specific changes needed
  • ‚ùå REVISE TITLE - Need to change topic or approach
üí° Tip: Take notes ng lahat ng suggestions. I-incorporate lahat sa next draft!
STEP 3: Writing Phase (Part 1)

‚úçÔ∏è Sumulat ng Chapter 1, 2, 3

üìñ CHAPTER 1: The Problem and Its Background

Ano ang ilalagay:

  • Introduction - Panimula, overview
  • Background of the Study - Context, setting, bakit importante
  • Literature Review - Ano ang sabi ng ibang researchers (30+ sources, 5-10 years old)
  • Theoretical Framework - Theories na base ng study (SDT, Constructivism, etc.)
  • Conceptual Framework - Diagram: INPUT ‚Üí PROCESS ‚Üí OUTPUT
  • Statement of the Problem - Research questions (numbered, specific)
  • Hypothesis (if applicable) - Expected outcomes
  • Significance of the Study - Sino ang makikinabang? Paano?
  • Scope and Delimitation - Ano ang saklaw? Ano ang hindi kasama?
  • Definition of Terms - Important terms, alphabetical order
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Gamitin ang APA 7th Edition para sa citations! Format: (Author, Year)

üî¨ CHAPTER 2: Research Methodology

Ano ang ilalagay:

  • Overview/Introduction - Short intro, restate research questions
  • Research Design - Experimental? Survey? Descriptive?
  • Population and Sample - Sino ang target? Ilan? Paano pipiliin?
  • Sampling Technique - Random? Purposive? Stratified?
  • Description of Respondents - Age, grade level, strand, etc.
  • Research Instrument - Questionnaire? Test? Observation checklist?
  • Data Gathering Procedure - Step-by-step kung paano mo kukunin ang data
  • Statistical Treatment - Anong formula/test gagamitin? (Mean, T-test, ANOVA, etc.)

Sample Research Instrument:

For Survey: Questionnaire with Likert Scale (1-5)

For Experimental: Pre-test and Post-test

For Qualitative: Interview guide, Observation checklist

üìä CHAPTER 3: (Wait, this is for AFTER data gathering!)

Sa Pre-Oral Defense: Chapter 1-2 + Research Instrument lang ang ipepresent

Chapter 3 (Results) - Gagawin mo AFTER ng data gathering

üí° Timeline:
Chapter 1-2 ‚Üí Pre-Oral ‚Üí Data Gathering ‚Üí Chapter 3-4 ‚Üí Final Defense
STEP 4: Pre-Oral Defense

üéØ Defend Chapters 1-2 + Research Instrument

üì¶ Requirements:

  • Chapter 1 (complete)
  • Chapter 2 (complete)
  • Research Instrument (questionnaire/test/guide)
  • 4 printed copies (3 days before defense)
  • PowerPoint presentation (15-20 mins)
‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: Kung WALANG research instrument, automatic CONDITIONAL ang decision! Kailangan may questionnaire ka o test instrument na ready na!

üëî Dress Code:

SCHOOL UNIFORM (Pre-oral lang, hindi pa corporate)

‚è∞ Sa Araw ng Defense:

  1. Opening prayer, introductions (5 mins)
  2. Your presentation (15-20 mins)
  3. Q&A with panelists (20-30 mins)
  4. Deliberation (lalabas kayo ng room)
  5. Announcement of results
üí° Defense Tips:
  • Practice presentation 3-5 times beforehand
  • TUMAYO while presenting, hindi uupo
  • Eye contact with panelists
  • Speak clearly and confidently
  • If hindi mo alam ang sagot, be honest: "That's a good point. I'll research more on that."

Possible Results:

92% above Minor revisions lang - proceed to data gathering!
83-91% Major revisions - need substantial changes
75-82% Conditional - may specific conditions to meet
STEP 5: Data Gathering

üìã Kumuha ng Data

Bago Magsimula:

  • Get approval from adviser
  • Prepare permission letters (kung may respondents from other schools)
  • Print enough copies ng questionnaire
  • Prepare informed consent forms

During Data Gathering:

  • Explain clearly sa respondents ang purpose ng study
  • Assure confidentiality and anonymity
  • Give enough time to answer (don't rush them)
  • Check if lahat ng questions nasagot
  • Thank them after!

Sample Timeline:

Week 1: Distribute questionnaires

Week 2: Collect responses, conduct interviews

Week 3: Encode data, tabulate results

After Gathering:

  • Encode all data (Excel o SPSS)
  • Compute statistics (mean, standard deviation, etc.)
  • Create tables and graphs
  • Get Statistician's Certification (kung required)
STEP 6: Writing Phase (Part 2)

‚úçÔ∏è Sumulat ng Chapter 3 at 4

üìä CHAPTER 3: Results and Discussion

Ano ang ilalagay:

  • Overview - Brief intro
  • Presentation of Data - Tables, graphs, figures
  • Analysis per Research Question - One by one, answer each problem
  • Interpretation - Ano ang meaning ng results? Agree ba sa theories?
  • Discussion - Compare with other studies, explain findings
üí° Format ng Tables:
Table number and title - ITAAS ng table
Figure number and title - IBABA ng figure

‚úÖ CHAPTER 4: Summary, Conclusions, Recommendations

Ano ang ilalagay:

  • Summary of Findings - Buod ng lahat ng nakita mong results
  • Conclusions - Sagot sa research questions, based on findings
  • Recommendations - Suggestions for:
    • Students
    • Teachers
    • School administrators
    • Future researchers
‚ö†Ô∏è Note: 3 subheadings lang ang kailangan sa Chapter 4! Wag dagdagan.
STEP 7: Final Defense

üèÜ Final Oral Defense - THE BIG DAY!

üì¶ Requirements (3 days before):

  • 4 complete copies ng FULL research paper (Chapter 1-4)
  • Complete preliminaries (Title page, Approval sheet, Abstract, etc.)
  • Complete appendices (Questionnaire, Tabulation, etc.)
  • PowerPoint presentation

üëî Dress Code:

CORPORATE ATTIRE (Smart casual o formal wear)

‚ö†Ô∏è Bawal:
  • Earrings (for men)
  • Dyed hair
  • Improper haircut
  • Slippers, tsinelas

Hindi ka padadefend kung hindi sumunod sa dress code!

üé§ Presentation (15-20 mins):

Ano ang ipepresent:

  1. Introduction and Background (2-3 mins)
  2. Statement of the Problem (1 min)
  3. Brief Methodology (2 mins)
  4. MAIN FOCUS: Results and Findings (8-10 mins)
  5. Conclusions and Recommendations (2-3 mins)
üí° Pro Tip: Focus sa findings! Yan ang main interest ng panel. Hindi mo na kailangang basahin lahat ng Chapter 1-2 kasi nabasa na nila.

‚ùì Q&A Phase:

Order ng Questioning:

  1. Panelist 1 (Content Expert) - questions about methodology, results
  2. Panelist 2 (Technical Expert) - questions about process, research design
